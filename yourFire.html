Title Cards and Animation

December 2024

academic


about
For this assignment, we had to create 3 title cards from 3 screenshots of a music video. I chose Magdalena Bay's "Secrets; Your Fire", and used this assignment as a method to test my flexibility in design thinking. We were also tasked with creating a short animation using one of our 3 title cards in Adobe AfterEffects; I chose the first in the series, which you can watch at the bottom right.

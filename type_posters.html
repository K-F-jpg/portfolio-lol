This was a 30-poster series made for Core Studio 1 my Sophomore year. We were instructed to create a series of posters that explored type as form, pattern, and texture; I have picked out a sample of each to scroll through in the document to the left. I have also included one of the colored posters we were required to make using a grid- I leaned into the digital aesthetic with the geometric sans-serif font. [1: Form, 2-3: Rhythm, 4-5: Texture] We had to construct our posters from a six-word sentence; I chose "World at my fingertips, so disconnected", modeled after the disconnect I felt having access to the internet 24/7 yet perpetually feeling out of touch with the world at large.

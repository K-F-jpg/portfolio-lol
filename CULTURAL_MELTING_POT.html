I was inspired to create this whilst studying for a marketing class and reading an excerpt describing America as a "cultural melting pot." It got me thinking about what a visual of that would entail. I plan for this to be a 2-poster series, with the first (featured here) reading "Take a dip into CULTURE!", and the second, "SPLASH! into culture."

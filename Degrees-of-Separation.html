Degrees of Separation

3/12/25


about
So basically, I created this after the events of yesterday evening. I had fallen asleep around 5PM, and missed my dad's invitation to get some food to eat since he was ordering out. I awoke to 3 missed calls from him and a few unread text messages stating that he was ordering food, alongside the menu of the place he ordered it from. After leaving my room to join them, I paused, thinking that "I shouldn't go down there" because I happened to fall asleep at the wrong time; this thought process inspired the piece since I felt like there were layers to my thinking, with the bakc of my mind and even layers between holding me back from doing what I wanted. I feel like this is a good indicator of my anxiety, in general, so I just wanted to portray it here.

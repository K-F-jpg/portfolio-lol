about
This piece was created on a whim, based around the idea of "WORMS" as "DOERS, MOVERS," and "SHAKERS" of the earth. I used the text element "W" to create blades of grass leading into the rest of the word "WORMS", and to encircle the worm/ text in the middle of the poster.

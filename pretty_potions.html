"Pretty Potions" is a series of posters centered around the idea of water as an all-powerful elixr for its multitude of benefits to one's health, skin, organ function, and more. In essence, it's a focus on water as a real-life "potion". This is a variant featuring text in perspective behind and in front of the central bottle, spelling out "pretty potions."
